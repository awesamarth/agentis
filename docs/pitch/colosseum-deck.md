# Agentis Colosseum Pitch Deck

One-liner: **Financial stack for AI agents**

Blurb:

Agentic commerce in crypto is accelerating. Agents are beginning to pay for APIs, hold balances, move funds, interact with protocols, and operate without a human approving every action.

On Solana, the primitives exist, but the operating layer is scattered. A builder has to stitch together wallets, x402/MPP payments, spending limits, private transfers, and yield integrations. Agentis brings those pieces into one platform: wallets, payments, policy enforcement, privacy, and yield for AI agents.
